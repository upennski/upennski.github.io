OFFICERS


PRESIDENT

CHRIS FOX
Chris is a Senior studying Mechanical Engineering. He definitely has the most steeze on the team with his helmet frog eyes (pictured) and impeccable dance moves. You can spot him easily since his big red pick up truck tends to be the only Alabama license plate parked at the slopes. He is most excited about ski season so he can cut his hair into a mullet. Beware of the beans and big burritos. BBBB “Can you follow cam me” “Always take off an accessory before leaving the house, just kidding”

VICE PRESIDENT

SARAH KLEIN
Sarah is a Junior studying Mathematical Economics. While she is proud of her Swedish heritage, don’t ask her to put your MALM© Bed Frame together. Small and mighty, she can go from 100-asleep real fast if there is a table or some arrangement of chairs anywhere in sight. While incredibly dexterous on the moguls and through the chutes, some have likened her jumping ability to that of a uh something that can’t jump. “My knee is fine it just feels a little mushy and weird” “frank....no” “chris....no” “jack....no”

ALPINE CAPTAIN

ALEXA MURRAY
Alexa “I skied at boarding school” Murray can tell you that she’s definitely the most athletic member of the team since she’s a 3 sPoRt aThLEte. She’s a Senior studying ESE, meaning this is her final year leading the women’s alpine team to victory because we actually do win that event. She warns, “Do not smile at me I will not smile back” “not everything’s a competition, just kidding”

ALPINE CAPTAIN

KIM AMELSBERG
Kim “I also skied at boarding school” Amelsberg is a Senior studying Biology. Although a Senior, this is only her second year on the team, but she quickly became a star by being very German. Even though we’ve never seen her race after a season ending concussion (very much not due to skiing) last year, we somehow decided that she’d be a good Alpine Captain due to her telling us that she’s pretty fast. “Stop punching me in the face Chris” “Those groomers were sick”

FREESTYLE CAPTAIN

EMMIE GOCKE
Emmie Gocke is a Junior and is premed studying Biology. This makes sense because she’s the most in touch with nature skier on the mountain, and is from a state that isn’t even real, Wyoming. Sometimes gluten free beverages inspire her to crowd surf, but most of the time she is landing a double twisty cork screw disaster turny. “It’s a nice coincidence nationals is in Jacksonhole because that’s where my house is” “Race camp should be in Jackson this year”

TREASURER

SARAH ENGELHARDT
Sarah Engelhardt is a Senior studying Chemical and Biomolecular Engineering. If you see someone on the mountain wearing braided pink tails tucked into a helmet it is likely Sarah. She has recently grown an extra inch that has graduated her into the world of adult skis. She’s the nicest person on the team, as long as you don’t mention that the Orioles have finished last in the league for the past 3 years. “I just like smiling, smiling is my favorite” “Inclusion is key” “Tune into my radio station!”

SOCIAL CHAIR

CAITLIN GUST
Caitlin Gust is a Senior studying economics. She is just one of the bros so don’t mess dude. She’s a west coast skier but she’s mainly here to end the day at 1 pm and have a good time. Don’t argue with her about football, or really anything because Caitlin will win even if she’s (somehow) wrong. She is known to USCSA as the best cup slapper on both sides of the Mississippi. “I will end all of you if we don’t make it back in time for Sunday night football. No you cannot have one more run.”

SOCIAL CHAIR

KEREN BARANOV
Keren Baranov is a Senior studying economics. After the slopes she wastes no time breaking it down to total bangerzzz at aprés ski. We’ve never seen her participate in an event but she’s really good at setting up mixers with the tennis team. She is probably way cooler than you. “You can’t sit with us” “Is that mean I can’t tell”

MEDIA AND MERCH

B KARP
B Karp is a Sophomore studying something sciency and techy, we don’t know what exactly because she can’t pronounce it. If she’s not screaming “Last Nights Mascara” in Chris Fox’s truck while having a laugh attack, she’s probably having a laugh attack after getting wrecked on a rail instead. She went to Nationals her first year AND claims she’s the best auxer on the mountain. Ski with her and you will quickly hear about whose outfits she thinks are the steeziest. “B fast or B Karp” *laughs*

RECRUITMENT

ZOVI KHRIMIAN
Zovi is a Visual Studies / Cinema and Media Junior. Her studies help explain why she has more style than anyone else on the mountain. Her impressive style is also apparent in her race techniques (pictured left) which have helped her slide into victory during her time on the team. As a proud resident of Queens, you can find her roasting people from Westchester County who say they’re from the city. “I am so happy, I didn’t get last place” “Is there a vegetarian option?”
